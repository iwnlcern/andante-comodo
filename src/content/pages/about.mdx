This site is a template demo. It uses Gustav Mahler as a public historical placeholder so the pages feel complete before you replace the identity layer.

The first files to edit are `src/config/site.mjs`, `src/config/collections.mjs`, `src/data/resume.ts`, and the entries under `src/content/`. Replace the portrait, scores, recordings, and prose before publishing a real site.

Media provenance is documented in `public/CREDITS.md`; licensing warnings for the demo recordings are in `NOTICE`.
